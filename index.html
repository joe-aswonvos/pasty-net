About Pasty Net

Cornwall, with its dramatic coastline and rugged beauty, is renowned for its ever-changing weather. While the sun often shines, those classic Cornish mists and sudden downpours can sometimes dampen the spirits. But fear not! This collection of Cornish mini-games is designed to keep you entertained and amused when the weather takes a turn for the worse. Whether you're sheltering from a summer shower in a cozy pub, or battling the winter gales at home, these games offer a delightful dose of Cornish charm and a welcome distraction from the elements.

From traditional pastimes like 'whack-a-mole' to quirky challenges inspired by Cornish folklore, these games celebrate the unique spirit of Cornwall. Whether you're a seasoned local or a curious visitor, you'll find something to pique your interest and test your skills. So grab a cuppa, settle in, and enjoy a taste of Cornwall, no matter what the weather may bring.
